How Expense Management App Works

  1. Group-based account system
    • First user (superuser) signs up â†’ creates a Group ID.
    • Acts as group admin.
  2. Multi-user under a group
    • Group admin can add multiple users.
    • All users in a group can add/view transactions & reports.
  3. Personal groups
    • A family member can also create their own separate group by signing up independently.
    • One person can belong to multiple groups (family + personal).
  4. Default categories for expenses
    • Predefined categories (Groceries, Food, Gym, Petrol, Shopping, etc.).
    • Users can add expenses under those categories.
  5. Monthly budget setting
    • Group or individual can define a monthly budget.
    • Not strict, just an estimate.
  6. Expense Reports
    • Track expenses, categories, and compare with monthly budget.
    • Helps in financial management.
